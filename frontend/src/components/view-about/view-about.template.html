Our buiness idea

CookItAll is an online recipe recommendation platform where people who are looking for cooking ideas can either search for recipes consisting only of the ingredients they have at home, or explore all available recipes on the platform by keyword filtering.

Value Proposition What do we offer

Customer Segment To whom are we offering our service

The main customer segments of CookItAll are students, professionals, families and the elderly who are looking for recipe ideas. Instead of buying a lot of ingredients for one recipe, our customers want to find the perfect recipe using what they already have at home.
